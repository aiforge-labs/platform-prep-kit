## External public sources (to be populated in milestones 1.2+)

Each week's `Read` section will cite specific public material. All sources used will be:

- Free to access (no paywall for core content)
- Licensed under permissive terms (CC-BY, Apache 2.0, MIT, public domain) — or summarized rather than reproduced where licensing is restrictive
- Attributed with author + URL

Planned source types:

- CNCF whitepapers and project documentation
- Official Kubernetes, Flux, ArgoCD, Crossplane, Backstage docs
- Google SRE Book / SRE Workbook (free online — CC-BY-NC-ND, summarize + link only)
- Team Topologies (book — reference summaries only, linked; no reproduction)
- PlatformCon and KubeCon conference talks (public YouTube)
- DORA research reports (Google Cloud — free PDFs)
- Internal Developer Platform reference material (`internaldeveloperplatform.org`, CNCF Platforms WG)
- Public blog posts from platform teams at Spotify, Netflix, Zalando, Thoughtworks, etc.

**What will never be cited or quoted**:

- Content from Turbot or Turbot customers
- Paywalled courses or books where redistribution is unclear
- Proprietary vendor deep-dives where the material is not publicly distributable
- Any material that requires a login to access
